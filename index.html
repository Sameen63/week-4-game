Family Guy Game

Game Rules

1) You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2) There are four Characters below. By clicking on a charactor you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3) You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above random number.

4) The value of each Charactor is hidden from you until you click on it.

5) Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Charactor.

6) Good Luck, Have Fun.
